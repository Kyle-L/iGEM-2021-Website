Title

Our team registered 40 parts within the iGEM registry, many of which are optimized for the photosynthetic chassis PCC 7942, a generally underrepresented but excellent chassis for future iGEM projects. Many of these parts have strong experimental evidence for functional operation. Overall, these parts made up the 4 plasmids our team used to transform cyanobacteria. We strongly recommend checking out our favorite parts list, as these parts were the best documented and have BioBrick compatibility!
