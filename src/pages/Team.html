Prinicipal Investigator and Instructors

Xin Wang

| Assistant Professor of Microbiology

Fun fact: I used to love novels but now I can only read non-fictions.

Xin Wang is an assistant professor of microbiology and serves as the primary principal investigator on Miami Universitys iGEM team. Wang has been with Miami University since 2017 after completing a post-doctoral fellowship at Texas A&M University and receiving his doctorate from the University of Hawaii at Mnoa in 2013. He is an expert in metabolic engineering, synthetic biology, and the metabolism of microbes. His research focuses on creating microbes for applications in energy production, medicine, and environmental protection. For more on Wang's research, checkout the Wang Group Microbiology website: https://www.wangxlab.com/

Luis Actis

| Professor and Chair of Microbiology

Luis Actis is a professor and chair of microbiology and serves as the secondary principal investigator on Miami Universitys iGEM team. After receiving his Ph.D. from the National University of Crdoba in Crdoba, Argentina has served as the chair of Miami Universitys microbiology department. His current research centers on the genetic, molecular and functional analyses of some of the factors responsible for the ability of the bacterium Acinetobacter baumannii to persist in medical environments and cause serious infections in humans.
